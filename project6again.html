Project 6: Data Portrait

Reflection

In this project, the objective was to create a data portrait out of a personal habit, spending a week to gather data to base our visualization on. I was tracking my habit of reading manga. It is rare that I don't read manga everyday. I usually read while eating, or as a form of personal rest.I tracked all the times I opened then closed chrome (in order to read manga), and then added the total amount of minutes per day.
