Project 5: Optical Illusion

Reflection

In this project, we were assigned to use for loops, and nested for loops to create patterns, as well as use if/else statements to animate the shapes for an optical illusion. In researching different optical illusions, I was entranced by several cyclical designs. I felt as though these especially resonated with my personal beliefs surrounding cyclicism in life. In believing so, I pursued a cyclical but not completely linear animation, as I included moments of chaos as shown by the more erratic speeds of rotation. I continued to exlore colors but I enjoyed the final blue-green natural scheme.
