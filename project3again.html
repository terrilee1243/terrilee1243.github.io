Project 3: Experimental Clock

Reflection

In this project, we were tasked to design an experimental clock- one that did not pertain to the second, minute, hours according to the 24 hour cycle. My experimental clock is how I experience the typical day of what comes into and out of my body- my very basic needs being met. It helps my keep track of when I need to meet basic needs of eating, drinking, breathing, and going to the bathroom. The moving circle is a representation of me going through the thoughts of all of my basic needs, and the elements passing through my body in the day is listed chronologically and feeling-wise from top to bottom.
